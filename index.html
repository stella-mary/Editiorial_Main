ABOUT US

Wisdom Publishers is formed by well-known scientists, researchers and academician from various domains of engineering with the motive of providing research to everyone in the world without any restrictions or barrier.

Wisdom Publishers is a multidisciplinary, open access (OA), applications-oriented, e-archival jornal that continuously presents the results of original research and development across all of research area of interest.

Wisdom Publishers will publish articles that are original, technically sound, clearly presented and high interest to readers, this journal is fully open access, which means that all contents freely available to users without charges. users are allowed to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of the articles published.

Wisdom Publishers proides a simple, an efficient and automated portal for researchers to upload their papers. Submitted papers will be reviewed by atleast two independent reviewers. All the articles will be screened for plagiarism before the review process. All the papers will be peer reviewed to maximize the content quality.
